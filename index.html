About

Small studio. Big standards.

The approach

Cosmivon is a solo-founded, research-driven product company based in Tamil Nadu, India. We build narrow software for narrow problems — starting with retention, because that's where most B2B SaaS companies quietly bleed.

Every product we ship is earned through research first, code second. No "AI-first" theatre. No investor-deck roadmaps. Just tools that founders use on Monday morning.

The stack

We use modern, boring infrastructure: TypeScript, Postgres, Next.js, Docker, and carefully chosen AI integrations where they add real value.

We run lean. We ship weekly. We prefer quality over quantity, focused products over bundled suites, and real customer conversations over growth hacks.
